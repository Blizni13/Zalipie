Painted Cottage - Zalipie

What is Zalipie?

Zalipie is perhaps one of the most beautiful villages in Poland, but surely the most colorful one. It is known for its tradition of painting special, folksy flower patterns. The whole village is painted and it charms people with its special atmosphere. There is no other place like that – the paintings are unique. And they are everywhere – not only houses are painted, but also barns, dog kennels, walls, furniture, fences, clothes, you name it!

Where is Zalipie?

Zalipie is located in Lesser Poland, in the South of Poland. It is very close to Krakow (something about 100 kilometers). So if you're in Krakow, the way will take something about hour and a half. If we look at google maps, we can see that it is between Krakow, Tarnow, Sandomierz and Czestochowa.
